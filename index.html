About

Hi, I'm Carlo.

I love capturing people's favorite moments in time and provide memories to look back on for the years to come. I photograph sports, Dines, couples and events in the Potchefstoom, North West area. My approach is simple: I look for real moments rather than stiff poses, and I edit toward how a scene actually felt rather than how a filter says it should look.

Every session is shot on location, in natural light where possible, and delivered as a curated set of high-resolution images you can print, share, and keep.
